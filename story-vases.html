Commissioned storytelling

Translate your memories into sculpted narratives

Story Vases are collaborative, one-of-a-kind commissions. Together we map the moments that matter most, then render them in carved motifs, layered glazes, and tactile surfaces that invite the hand as much as the eye.

We all hold cherished memories—stories that we treasure and share, reliving them time and again. These experiences, whether they are the dawning of a love story or the thrill of a daring adventure, become woven into the fabric of our lives. From the quiet wonder of a starlit night to the exuberant joy of a childhood moment, these memories shape who we are and remind us of where we've been.

Imagine capturing your most treasured stories on a timeless piece of art. My handcrafted pottery vases are more than beautiful objects; they are canvases waiting to be adorned with the narratives that define you. I work closely with you to translate your story into a captivating visual composition. Whether it's the vibrant hues of a childhood memory, the soft glow of a starlit night, or the intricate details of a cherished heirloom, your unique narrative will come to life in clay.

Sunrise Reverie

A dawn-lit palette washes across the vessel, capturing the quiet stillness of a morning ritual shared between generations.

Trail of Discovery

Layered carvings map the winding path of a solo journey, punctuated with motifs that mark lessons learned along the way.

Seaside Keepsake

Glossy blues and soft textures echo tide pools and evening light, commemorating a quiet proposal by the water's edge.

Embrace the stories that make you, and let's transform them into a timeless heirloom.
